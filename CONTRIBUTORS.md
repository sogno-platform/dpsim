# Contributors

We would like to thank all contributors who have helped develop and improve DPsim over the years, whether through code, research, testing, or feedback.

This list is based on deduplicated commit authors and highlights main contributors.
For a complete, automatically updated list use the command `git shortlog -sne`.

Are you a new contributor? Add yourself in the list when you send your PR. Are we missing you or your data is not correct? Send a PR with the change.
